Who are we?

Low Carbon Trade is a platform that will offer a spot market for the voluntary carbon offset market . Our mission is to provide a blockchain based ecosystem that connects buyers and sellers of carbon offsets within this new exploding market that is forecasted to grow in volume by 50 times by 2030.

Why is our product needed?

The voluntary carbon offset market is an increasingly important source of financing for environmentally friendly projects. By investing in carbon offsets, investors can support projects that capture Co2 from the atmosphere while also earning a financial return.

What do we provide?

As a platform for trading in the carbon offset spot market, we provide a secure and transparent environment where buyers and sellers can negotiate trades. We offer a number of unique features that set us apart from other trading platforms in the carbon offset market. These include:

  • Opening the carbon offset market to the new digital era by tokenizing the assets on our private blockchain ecosystem.
  • Offering data aggregation services investors can use a single source of truth to make financial decisions.
  • Offering a robust financial solution that allow us to adapt to future market needs by building on top of our existing solutions.
  • Opening the door to new DeFi solutions.

Our Motive an Purpose

At the core of Low Carbon Trade lies a strong passion for blockchain technology and its potential to transform the financial industry. We believe that the decentralization and transparency offered by blockchain can foster trust, security, and efficiency in financial transactions.

Our purpose is to build a company that is not only financially successful, but also socially and environmentally responsible. We aim to create a platform that enables individuals and institutions to invest in projects that have a positive impact on the environment, while still providing solid financial returns by leveraging blockchain technology. We believe that by directing investment towards environmentally conscious projects and providing incentives for businesses to adopt sustainable practices, we can drive positive change and contribute to the fight against climate change.
